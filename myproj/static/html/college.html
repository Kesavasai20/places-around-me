COLLEGE:

SRI VENKATESWARA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING


Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering and Technology, established in 1998 is affiliated to JNTUA, Anantapur and approved by AICTE, NBA. SVCET offers B.Tech courses at the undergraduate level and offers MBA, MCA and M.Tech courses in various disciplines at the postgraduate level. Admission to the courses offered will be done based on the candidate’s performance in the entrance examination. Some of the top recruiters that recruit SVCET’s students include IBM, Cognizant, Infosys, Wipro, Polaris etc.
